About HomeTuitions

HomeTuitions is a simple and effective platform created to bridge the gap between students and qualified tutors. Whether you're a student seeking help in math, science, languages, or arts — or a tutor looking to share your knowledge — this platform is for you.

Our mission is to provide personalized, accessible, and affordable education by connecting learners with tutors right in their local area.

Built with love using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this site is designed to be lightweight, responsive, and user-friendly. As we grow, we aim to add more features like tutor reviews, ratings, and booking systems to enhance the learning experience.

Thank you for being a part of our journey toward better learning opportunities for everyone.
